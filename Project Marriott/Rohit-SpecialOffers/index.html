Frequently Asked Questions

What is Marriott Bonvoy Offers™?

Looking for unbeatable hotel deals? Discover exclusive offers at over 30 hotel brands and 10,000 global destinations within the Marriott Bonvoy Offers Hub.

How can I find the best hotel discounts?

Join Marriott Bonvoy® for access to exclusive member rates and benefits at participating properties worldwide.

How do I redeem an offer?

To redeem an offer, log into your account, choose your desired deal, and follow the steps to book.

What are Member Exclusive offers?

Member Exclusive offers are special promotions available only to Marriott Bonvoy members.

What are Elite Exclusive offers?

Elite Exclusive offers are additional perks and promotions for our elite-tier members.

What are Bonus Point offers?

Bonus Point offers allow you to earn extra points when booking certain promotions or stays.
